Our Story

A taste of the Mediterranean, in the heart of Alger

La Paella Alger offers fresh seafood dishes and authentic paella in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

From the sizzling pan to your table, every dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a deep respect for tradition. Join us for a meal that brings together the warmth of Algerian hospitality and the soul of Spanish cuisine.
